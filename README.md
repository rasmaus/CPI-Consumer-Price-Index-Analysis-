## Key Findings

* 2019 recorded the highest inflation (~8%), driven by fuel price increases and supply chain disruptions
* Highest food inflation observed in June 2022
* Lowest food inflation observed in December 2022 and February 2023
* Post-COVID inflation showed higher volatility compared to the pre-COVID period
